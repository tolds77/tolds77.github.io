Apollo’s 5th Spider-Man Birthday

Apollo absolutely loves Spider-Man. Since we recently redecorated his room with a custom Spider-Man mural, it only felt right to base his 5th birthday on what he loves the most—his boho Spider-Man room and his favorite superhero.

Custom Party Palette & Cake Design

I designed a custom color palette inspired by his room’s aesthetic and sent it to the cake designer. She captured my vision perfectly, creating a stunning, comic-inspired cake that tied in seamlessly with the decorations.

DIY Balloons & Decorations

I took charge of the balloon installations and party decorations to match the theme. The setup included a Spider-Man backdrop with cascading balloon garlands in deep reds, navy blues, and golden yellows.

Spider-Man Bounce House & A Rainy Day Celebration

Apollo had a Spider-Man bounce house at his party, and despite the pouring rain, he had the absolute best time celebrating with his friends. Rain or shine, the excitement never stopped!
